About Us

The Katalyst projects are about creating self-sufficiency in communities in Fiji and Australia.

Who We Are?

Let's say more about us

We strive for change by providing funding for essential services, skills training and infrastructure. By developing small local businesses we aim to provide ongoing employment opportunities for locals in Fiji and attend to the basic needs of our local community.

In Fiji , by introducing businesses that will employ villagers and make use of the natural resources of their surrounding environment, the Katalyst Foundation aims to teach members of the community how to attain and maintain prosperity, build sustainability of their community and support their local community activities. Which will allow the employees of its businesses to support themselves within a framework of environmentally friendly, self-funding enterprises in years to come.

Katalyst Overview

These businesses will make use of the natural resources of their surrounding environment and teach members of the community how to attain and maintain prosperity, build sustainability of their community and support their local community activities.

Katalyst Fiji

Katalyst foundation Fiji, works differently to Australia, where it uses social business (businesses for another purpose other than money) to achieve a change in the future of the Fijians.

Fiji Donors

Further, the profits generated from the Katalyst businesses will be reinvested into the existing communities, and crucial to long lasting sustainability, into the communities.

Katalyst Overview

  • Katalyst centres on the philosophy of creating a self-sufficient economy in the less fortunate parts of Fiji, by donating funds, services, skills training and infrastructure to make lasting changes one village at a time.

    Rob Cromb, C.E.O

  • Central to the Katalyst model, is the development of small, local businesses that will provide ongoing employment for Fijians and help develop and stimulate a local economy.

Katalyst Australia

In Australia, the Katalyst foundation, strives to support many already existing community and charity programs with financial aid and resources. Fundamentally we strive to give the basic human rights to all people in the community that are less fortunate.

Katalyst Food Van
Katalyst organises the hire of a food van, equipped with food and donated clothes, to feed and clothe the homeless one night per week throughout parts of St Kilda, Melbourne.

School Bike Program

Bass Straight Special Needs School Bike Program.View site

School Bike Program
Another project the organisation is involved in is the donation of bicycles to school children in Fiji. Together with the students and teachers of bass coast specialist school in Wonthaggi, old bicycles donated to the foundation are hand restored and reconditioned for use by Fijian school children.

SOS

Through donations and resources Katalyst assist st. Joseph's outreach program in helping homeless in need of transitional accommodation.View site

SOS
Through donations and resources Katalyst assist st. Joseph's outreach program in helping homeless in need of transitional accommodation. SOS is a crisis centre for people in need of emergency accommodation or transitional accommodation, where they strive to not only house them but feed, console and help re-enter the community as successfully as possible.
